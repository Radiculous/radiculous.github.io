Brief Backgrounder

What is Swedish design?

In a nutshell, Swedish design is functional and modern.

In Dieter Rams'own words:

Design is how it works

The focus on function is embedded with the Swedes' design ethos.

IKEA put Sweden into the world stage through its use of affordable, functional furniture. It rattled the furniture industry by providing beautiful furniture at accessible prices.

The swedish influence permeate₫ throughout the world. It made it possible to own beautiful things without spending an arm and a leg.

Apart from its design sensibilities, Sweden is also known for its vast lakes, lush forests and breathtaking views.

What's in a name?

Bygga – Swedish for "to build, to set up, to raise" sets the precedent on what the organization is about, Bygga is about action.

With a primary focus on community-building, Bygga Realty aims to raise the standard of what a community should look like.

To Bygga, a community should flourish, thrive and become self-sufficient.

Their first project, The Aspen Place sits atop one of the Philippines' highest cities.

An identity you can smell

Synesthesia is the neural phenomenon that causes one sense to be experienced through another like for example, seeing sound, or perhaps tasting color.

The power of this idea we aim to transport with our identity. We want our identity to remind the market of Sweden.

They say the sense of smell is the most powerful sense of all. Smells can transport you across time and space. Smell can affect emotions.

When you think of Sweden you’re immediately reminded of the strong cedar smell, maybe it’s because you’re being reminded of IKEA, and that’s OK.

The aim of our identity is to remind our customers of Sweden, because of its pleasant visual appeal.

The Typeface

The aim of the identity is to keep it organic and relatable. As much as possible we try to avoid hard edges to let our eyes glide and wander.

Still sticking with our wood and forest theme, We call on the help of Knockout, a typeface created by Hoefler & Co. that is based on wooden type used for posters.

The warmth of the letterforms gives it a familiar look but the typeface family is large enough to be flexible in multiple applications.

The Color Scheme : Nature Lover

Color is the most powerful tool we have in our design, before we get the mark noticed, color is what our eyes first percieve.

Let's use our natural proclivity to the color green to get noticed.

One way to do it is to extract the most approachable color scheme.

Where better to get inspiration than from mother nature herself?

Our Natural instict is to veer towards blue

Blue is peaceful, blue is calming but at the same time blue is familiar.

Pepper in some green because green is go, green is nature and green is home.

Conclusion

We create Bygga Realty and Development's identity as a testament to where Bygga stands --the intersection of Swedish design and the tranquilty of nature. It's a beacon of innovation and simplicity

The elegant minimalism of the design speaks volumes through its understated complexity, reflecting the meticulous care and thoughtful planning that Bygga invests in every community it builds.

The monogram is more than a symbol; it is a promise – a promise of a developer that resonates with the serenity of Swedish landscapes and the warmth of Swedish design. It represents the merging of two worlds: the robust, reliable nature of construction and the delicate, deliberate strokes of aesthetic refinement.

It is an identity that does not merely represent a brand but invites an experience. It beckons the senses to engage.

Bygga's logo is not just seen but felt, not just observed but lived. It is a symbol that moves forward with purpose and promise, much like Bygga Realty & Development Corporation itself.
