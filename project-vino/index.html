Typography

If the voice is how we speak, the typography is how we want to be heard. Every font type, every style has a corresponding voice.

When auditioning appropriate typefaces for identity, what we look for are voice and tone. Does the typeface convey the right voice? Does it read the right way? How is the tone?

Is it authoritative and imposing or is it sweet and subtle? Does it whisper or scream?

All these nuances have to be considered before delving in and proposing a typeface.
